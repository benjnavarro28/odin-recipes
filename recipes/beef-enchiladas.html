Beef Enchilada Casserole (gluten-free and dairy-free)

Special thanks to my mom for creating this recipe. :)

Ingredients

  • 1lb. Fully-cooked ground beef
  • 9 white corn tortillas
  • 2 14oz. cans of Hatch brand Red Enchilada Sauce
  • 2 packages of Daiya Dairy-Free Cheddar Cheese Shreds

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Pour a small layer of enchilada sauce in a 9x13 pan.
  3. Place 3 tortillas in the pan to make a layer of tortillas
  4. Add half of the beef onto the tortilla layer
  5. Pour enough cheese to cover the layer
  6. Cover the layer with a thin layer of enchilada sauce
  7. Use a spoon to press down the layer
  8. Add 3 more tortillas
  9. Add another thin layer of enchilada sauce
  10. Use a spoon to spread the enchilada sauce on the layer, then use the spoon to press down the layer
  11. Pour the remaining beef onto the layer
  12. Cover the layer with cheese
  13. Add 3 more tortillas
  14. Pour the remaining sauce
  15. You can optionally add other desired toppings, such as onions
  16. Pour the remaining cheese
  17. Bake for 25 minutes
  18. Let stand for 5 minutes then serve
